About Me

Hello, I’m Anshu Vashishta, a qualified integrative counsellor with over 3 years of experience. I offer online counselling for young people (16+) and adults.

I provide a calm, supportive, and non-judgemental space where you can explore your thoughts and feelings at your own pace. My approach is flexible and tailored to your individual needs, helping you feel heard, understood, and supported.
