About Me

Hello, I’m Anshu Vashishta, a qualified integrative counsellor offering compassionate online support for anxiety, trauma, emotional wellbeing, stress, and life challenges.

I work with young people (16+) and adults.

My approach is flexible and tailored to your individual needs, helping you feel heard, understood, and supported in a calm, non-judgemental space.
